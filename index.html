About ROBOVANZA

ROBOTICS AND INNOVATION CLUB of CBIT has come into collaboration with IEEE CBIT to bring out the best of its technicalities and innovation. We present you a bigger and a better version of our event “ ROBOVANZA-2K19” conducted at CBIT. This fest emphasizes to encourage all the enthusiasts who want to seek knowledge in the art of ROBOTICS. The members of our club have been striving to be the best in this ART while polishing their skills everywhere possible. Here, we provide the best platform to showcase their talents. ROBOTICS requires high level of intellect and thinking, and this is what the members of our club posses. We wish to broaden our horizons through ROBOVANZA where we believe :-

“Knowledge when shared, becomes Wisdom”
So we wish to see you all on 13th and 14th of September 2019
Come explore and share.
